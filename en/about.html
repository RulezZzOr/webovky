Our Story

Cloudpeakify s.r.o. is the vision of one man, Richard Skácel. Richard is a certified Google Cloud Architect, a specialist in Azure, and an expert in VMware. His vision is simple: to provide not only top technical expertise but also strategic consulting that helps companies find the right path in the digital world. Cloudpeakify s.r.o. is not just another IT company. It is a commitment to your success.
